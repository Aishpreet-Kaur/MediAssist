ANXIETY & STRESS
----------------
Symptoms: Excessive worry, restlessness, irritability, racing thoughts, difficulty concentrating, muscle tension, sleep problems, rapid heartbeat, sweating, trembling, shortness of breath, GI issues.

Immediate Relief for Anxiety or Panic Attack:
1. 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale 4 counts, hold 7, exhale 8. Repeat 4 times.
2. 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding: Name 5 things you see, 4 you touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.
3. Cold water on face or wrists activates the dive reflex and slows heart rate.
4. Progressive muscle relaxation: tense each muscle group for 5 sec then release.

Long-term Home Management:
1. Regular exercise: 30 min/day significantly reduces anxiety hormones.
2. Limit caffeine and alcohol: both worsen anxiety.
3. Consistent sleep schedule: sleep deprivation amplifies anxiety.
4. Journaling: write worries down to externalize them.
5. Limit news and social media consumption.

Herbal Support:
1. Ashwagandha: adaptogen that reduces cortisol, 300-500mg extract daily.
2. Brahmi (Bacopa): traditional brain tonic, reduces anxiety.
3. Chamomile tea: mild calming effect.
4. Lavender aromatherapy: inhale or apply diluted to temples.

When to See a Doctor:
- Anxiety interfering with daily work, relationships, or functioning.
- Panic attacks occurring regularly.
- Using alcohol or substances to cope.
- Thoughts of self-harm: seek help immediately, iCall: 9152987821.

DEPRESSION (LOW MOOD)
---------------------
Symptoms: Persistent sadness or emptiness more than 2 weeks, loss of interest in previously enjoyed activities, changes in appetite or weight, sleep problems, fatigue, difficulty concentrating, feelings of worthlessness, thoughts of death or suicide.

Note: Depression is a medical condition, not a character flaw or weakness.

Self-Care Strategies:
1. Behavioral activation: schedule small achievable activities even when unmotivated.
2. Sunlight exposure: 20-30 minutes of morning sunlight boosts serotonin.
3. Exercise: even 15-minute walks have measurable antidepressant effects.
4. Social connection: isolation worsens depression, reach out to one person daily.
5. Omega-3 rich foods (fish, walnuts, flaxseed) support brain health.
6. Saffron (Kesar): small studies show mood-lifting effects, add few strands to warm milk.

When to See a Doctor:
- Symptoms lasting more than 2 weeks.
- Inability to carry out daily activities.
- Thoughts of suicide or self-harm: CALL iCall 9152987821 or Vandrevala Foundation 1860-2662-345 (24/7).

INSOMNIA / SLEEP PROBLEMS
--------------------------
Symptoms: Difficulty falling asleep, waking up frequently, waking too early, not feeling rested, daytime fatigue, mood irritability.

Home Remedies:
1. Consistent schedule: sleep and wake at same time daily, even weekends.
2. No screens 30-60 minutes before bed.
3. Keep room cool, dark, and quiet.
4. No caffeine after 2 PM.
5. Warm milk with honey or turmeric at bedtime.
6. Chamomile or valerian root tea.
7. Ashwagandha capsule at night: reduces cortisol.
8. 4-7-8 breathing to induce sleep.

OTC Medicines:
- Melatonin 0.5-3mg: take 30 minutes before bed, short-term use only.

When to See a Doctor:
- Insomnia lasting more than 3 weeks.
- Loud snoring or breathing pauses (possible sleep apnea).
- Insomnia with depression or anxiety.
